Appcon Technologies (Appcon Technologies GmbH) is an R&D engineering company specializing in sensorless motor control, power electronics, and embedded software for BLDC and PMSM motors. Founded in 2004, headquartered in Tunisia. The company develops hardware inverters and software algorithms that eliminate or reduce the need for position sensors in motor drives.

What the company does: Appcon offers consulting, hardware design, software development, model-based design, and prototyping. Products include DMK (measurement kit for motor testing), LCI (low-cost inverter for PMSM), and AMC (software-only sensorless control).

Problem solved: Motor drives traditionally require position sensors (encoders, resolvers), which add cost and complexity. Appcon's sensorless solutions achieve comparable performance without the sensor, reducing system cost and improving reliability.

Why unique: Appcon combines deep expertise in field-oriented control (FOC), sensorless observers, and inverter design with a product portfolio spanning measurement (DMK), hardware (LCI), and software (AMC) for white goods, power tools, automotive, and e-mobility.
